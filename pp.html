PRIVACY POLICY

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION

SecureConnect collects minimal information necessary to process orders. This may include:

  • Contact email for order confirmation
  • Shipping address for delivery purposes
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses for payment processing

2. DATA USAGE

We use collected information exclusively for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Communicating about order status
  • Providing customer support when requested

3. DATA RETENTION

We retain order information for 90 days after fulfillment, after which all personally identifiable information is permanently deleted from our systems.

4. INFORMATION SHARING

SecureConnect does not share, sell, or trade customer information with any third parties except:

  • Shipping carriers necessary for product delivery
  • When required by law through proper legal channels

5. SECURITY MEASURES

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect customer information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure communication channels
  • Regular security audits of our systems

6. COMMUNICATION SECURITY

We recommend using encrypted communication channels when contacting us. Our support team is trained in privacy best practices.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Our website uses minimal technical cookies required for functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics services.

8. POLICY CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. CONTACT

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@secureconnectphones.com using secure communication methods.
